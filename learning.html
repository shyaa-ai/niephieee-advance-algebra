About the Course

I learned how to understand and work with the fundamental ideas that make up more advanced math. I became familiar with variables, expressions, simple equations, and how to combine like terms correctly. I also learned the importance of following steps carefully, especially when solving equations or simplifying expressions. These lessons helped me build confidence because they showed me that even complex problems can be solved when I understand the basics. Overall, the basic lessons gave me a strong foundation and made Algebra feel more manageable and less intimidating.
